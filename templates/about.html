R.E.S.T

Rest,Explore through Space and Time

R.E.S.T is a 3d Arcade and adventure game where you race and explore through Indian locations of historical and geographical importance. It is a tribute to the lore and culture of our country via some advanced and trending technologies such as multiplayer racing and open world game dev, with an interesting storyline.

Locations


Harappan Civilization
Located in the Indus river valley, Harappan Civilization was the centre of one of the core civilizations of our nation. Get ready to get your mind blow by looking at the disciplined architecture approach and modern day problem solving techniques.
Himalayan range
The vast range of the snow-clad cliffs is waiting to be raced and explored through! Witness the shivering beauty and interact with the natives, tourists and the nature itself to calm your curiousity.
Sindhudurg Fort
The ultimate defense system of the west, the naval behemoth of the Marathas, and pride of Maharashtra is now here to give you chills while you explore its scenic yet tactic beauty! Don't miss the ecstasy and bravery of this magnificent monument.
